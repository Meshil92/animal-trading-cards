Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky Has a Wild, Wolf-Like Appearance

  • Weight‎:‎35 to 60 pounds
  • Height‎: ‎1 foot, 8 inches to 1 foot, 11 inches tall
  • Life Span‎: ‎12 to 15 years
  • Dog Breed Group‎: ‎Working Dogs

The Siberian Husky is a beautiful dog breed with a thick coat that comes in a multitude of colors and markings.
